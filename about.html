About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to Eve & Kids Adventures, your ultimate travel guide to the most breathtaking destinations across the globe. Whether you're a thrill-seeker, a nature lover, or someone looking for a serene escape, we've got something for everyone!

Mission

Our mission is simple: to inspire your next adventure. We aim to provide travelers with the best recommendations, insider tips, and destination highlights to make your journey unforgettable.

Our Values

We believe that travel connects people, broadens horizons, and creates memories that last a lifetime. We are passionate about promoting sustainable tourism and supporting local communities through travel.

Ready to embark on your next adventure? Let Eve & Kids Adventures guide the way!

Meet the team

Our team is made up of passionate travelers, writers, and creators who share one common goal: inspiring others to explore the world. Get to know the faces behind Eve & Kids Adventures and the stories that drive us to bring you the best travel experiences.

"The world is a book, and those who do not travel read only one page.”

Emmanuel Omwata

Founder & Travel Enthusiast

With a deep love for exploring hidden gems and experiencing diverse cultures, Emmanuel founded Eve & Kids Adventures to share his journey with fellow travelers. His favorite destination? The picturesque streets of Paris, where every corner feels like a masterpiece waiting to be discovered.

Having visited over 30 countries, Emmanuel has trekked through the lush jungles of Costa Rica, marveled at the ancient wonders of Egypt, and soaked in the serene beauty of the Maldives. Whether it's savoring street food in Bangkok, hiking in the Swiss Alps, or uncovering local traditions in Kenya, he thrives on connecting with the world through its people, diverse culture, stories, and landscapes.

When he's not traveling, Emmanuel loves documenting his adventures through photography and storytelling, inspiring others to step out of their comfort zones and explore the world. He’s always planning his next journey—maybe it’ll be chasing the Northern Lights in Iceland or diving into the vibrant cultures of South America!


Eve Anunda

Content Creator & Photographer

Eve captures the beauty of every destination through her lens, transforming moments into timeless memories. Her breathtaking images bring Eve & Kids Adventures to life, inspiring wanderers to chase new horizons and uncover the hidden magic of the world.

From the golden dunes of the Sahara to the serene beaches of Bali, Eve’s photography tells stories of adventure, culture, and connection. One of her most unforgettable experiences? Camping under the dazzling Northern Lights in Iceland, where she watched the sky come alive in a symphony of color.

Beyond her passion for photography, Eve loves connecting with locals, trying new cuisines, and immersing herself in the traditions of every destination. With her camera always in hand, she's dedicated to inspiring others to see the world through her unique perspective, one stunning image at a time.
